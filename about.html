About us

When Bee9 Design was founded, it was on the idea of creating unique furniture that actively improves life. That meant raw materials, real craftsmen, and a commitment to trying new ideas without sacrificing quality. Bee9 Design furniture isn't mass-produced; every new design is a result of months of work, all in service of creating furniture and accessories that enhance daily life.
