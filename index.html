Design In Ply

Welcome to Design In Ply, we are a bespoke furniture design studio and workshop. Using plywood as our primary material for our creations. We offer a complete service from design, to manufacturing and final fitting. Working with customers in domestic and commercial settings. Our pieces are all bespoke to the clients needs and styles. Whether its a home office (which have become more important these days), beatiful storage, one off piece of furniture or a stylish and practical kitchen, we can take care of it for you. For commercial projects, anything from full office fit outs to display units, we you it covered. Please get in touch to discuss any projects you have in mind.

What we Do

We communticate with the client throughout the design process to make sure it is meeting there expectations. We then get to making, using a mix off state of the art technologies and hand crafted techniques, to create beautiful practical and robust furniture. We have created many differnt types of furniture from full bespoke kitchens, one off pieces like chairs and sideboards, and complete office suites for the commercial sector. We only use high quality materials, specialising in plywood.

Why We Do it

Design In Ply was founded by Mark, a furniture designer and cabinet maker, whos passion for beautiful design and joinery fuels the companies ethos. To create bespoke pieces of furniture that stand the test of time, both in its design and consturction.

We use plywood for many reasons, it is versatile, strong and has a clean modern asthetic. Made by layering thin wood fibre sheets together in opposite directions, creating a strong stable material. It is also very sustainable, using more of the wood from a tree that traditional, solid woods. Using plywoods made from trees like birch which are fast growing and strong, also helps to reduce its envoirmental impact.
